# SEC Competitor List Mining

What it did: used competitor tables inside SEC filings to uncover hidden public and private suppliers around known winners.

Skill: treat filings as supply-chain graphs rather than static company descriptions.

Learnable move: open a known leader's 10-K, extract competitors, map every competitor into the same customer and process graph.
