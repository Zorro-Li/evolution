# x-creator-openai-newsroom-official-metrics

`@OpenAINewsroom` is now tracked as OpenAI's official corporate/product communications source. It is useful for adoption metrics, methodology caveats, product-use narratives, Codex audience expansion, and AI infrastructure/capex narratives.

## Durable Rule

When OpenAI Newsroom appears in X/Twitter research, classify posts across six surfaces:

1. Adoption metrics: usage growth, daily-user composition, geography, engagement.
2. Method caveats: anonymized data, first-name inference, sample scope, visible chart labels.
3. Product proof: quoted users, education examples, brand kits, creative workflows, office workflows.
4. Audience expansion: Codex for ChatGPT users, non-developer positioning, everyday-work positioning.
5. Infrastructure narrative: Stargate, Abilene, compute capacity, skilled trades, local investment, responsible infrastructure.
6. Corporate source graph: `@OpenAI`, `@ChatGPTapp`, `@OpenAIDevs`, `openai.com/news`.

## Jarvis Implication

Use OpenAI Newsroom posts as primary signals for public OpenAI metrics and corporate strategy. Convert adoption numbers into dashboards, convert quoted workflows into local tests, and convert infrastructure narratives into AI capex and policy watch items.
