## Material Selection Rules

- Prefer stories with clear operational transfer: planning, evidence, risk, collaboration, governance, verification, memory.
- Prefer source families over isolated quotes when exact wording is unnecessary.
- Avoid turning every proverb into absolute command; use trigger conditions and failure modes.
- For public output, cite source family and provide links to public text entry points.
- For Agent rules, phrase the lesson as observable behavior: "when X, do Y, verify Z."
