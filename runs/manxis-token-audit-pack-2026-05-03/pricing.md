# ManXis Token Audit Pricing

## Pilot

| Plan | Price | Scope | Goal |
|---|---:|---|---|
| First 3 pilots | $500 | 1 workflow, 1-2 traces, 1 report, 1 call | 换取真实 trace、反馈和可匿名 case。 |
| Standard audit | $1,500-$2,500 | 1 workflow, 3-5 traces, before/after benchmark, patch plan | 验证付费和节省空间。 |
| Team audit | $5,000-$10,000 | 2-3 workflows, trace import, engineering workshop | 找团队预算 owner。 |

## Success Fee Option

| Metric | Formula |
|---|---|
| Monthly baseline cost | daily tasks × baseline cost per task × 30 |
| Monthly savings | monthly baseline cost × savings rate |
| ManXis fee | monthly savings × 10%-30% |

## Example

| Metric | Value |
|---|---:|
| Daily long-agent tasks | 100 |
| Baseline cost per task | $3 |

## First Ask

Offer a $500 private audit to the first serious design partners. The commercial objective is one paid proof point plus permission to use an anonymous case.
