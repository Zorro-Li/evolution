## Purpose

Use `@beefnoode` as a live operating source for Chinese AI relay/API infrastructure, small AI product execution, ecommerce image generation, B2B AI services, and creator-led distribution.

## Workflow

1. Capture Beefapi product links, launch posts, progress updates, and pricing/reliability claims.
2. Classify posts by AI relay/API, ecommerce image generation, enterprise AI development, channel strategy, community growth, commercial deals, or founder-network building.
3. Extract quantitative operating metrics: users, revenue, community size, follower growth, deal count, delivery date, and company-registration status.
4. Map integration names such as Openclaw, Hermes, CC switch, Cursor, Alipay, QQ, Telegram, Xiaohongshu, Douyin, and WeChat Channels.
5. Convert his monthly operating updates into Jarvis business diagnostics: wedge, distribution, retention, support load, and next product extension.
