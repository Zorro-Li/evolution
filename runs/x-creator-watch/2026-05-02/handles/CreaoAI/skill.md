# Skill Distillation: @CreaoAI

## Purpose

Use `@CreaoAI` as a product and community source for super-agent workflows, saved agents, scheduled execution, recurring automation, and agent-system infrastructure vocabulary.

## Workflow

1. Capture profile, pinned product post, recent product education posts, community links, and `@CreaoBuilds` quotes.
2. Classify posts by product positioning, live build, saved agent, scheduled run, trigger, memory, file/runtime component, community demo, contest, hackathon, or channel-native automation.
3. Extract the workflow unit: user request, generated artifact, saved agent, schedule/trigger, output destination, and repeated task.
4. Convert examples into Jarvis reusable patterns: task definition, agent packaging, schedule, result callback, and source ledger entry.
5. Track engagement and event context separately because large product launches and small community demos serve different purposes.

## Failure Modes

- Treating product positioning as evidence of execution.
- Missing the schedule/trigger boundary that turns a chat answer into recurring work.
- Losing community examples that show what users actually build.
