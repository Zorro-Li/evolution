# Skill Distillation: @emollick

## Purpose

Use Ethan Mollick as a source for AI adoption frameworks, executive perception, education, innovation, startups, and organizational implications of new AI capabilities.

## Workflow

1. Capture the profile, pinned resource post, One Useful Thing links, Wharton GAIL links, and recent timeline posts.
2. Classify posts by adoption framework, education, workplace practice, executive perception, agent impact, model-race narrative, infrastructure/capex, or AI discourse quality.
3. Preserve resource links and bookmark-heavy posts as durable references.
4. Translate each claim into practical implications for Jarvis, HumanOS, education content, business diagnosis, or company research.
5. Track how X narratives move into executive questions and business planning.

## Failure Modes

- Treating one viral post as a framework without checking his resource links.
- Missing the distinction between actual adoption data and discourse commentary.
- Ignoring how X/LinkedIn narratives affect executives.
