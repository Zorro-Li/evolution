## What This Account Does

## Strength

1. Product launches are framed as executable behavior changes: build with GPT-5.5, migrate workflow to Codex, import configs, connect apps, choose roles, try prompts.
2. Developer activation is tied to incentives: DevDay early tickets and ChatGPT Pro rewards turn product usage into public submission loops.
3. Metrics and adoption claims are used as confidence signals: GPT-5.5 launch strength, API revenue growth, Codex revenue doubling, and enterprise demand.
