## What This Account Does

`@KobeissiLetter` is a global capital-markets commentary account. The visible batch turns macro data, liquidity signals, ETF flows, energy prices, tech labor data, crypto reserve behavior, geopolitics, and regulatory events into chart-backed market narratives.

## Strength

1. It converts macro data into fast, readable market hooks with a chart or image.
2. It links real-world data to asset flows: AI buildout to infrastructure ETFs, tax receipts to TGA liquidity, AI capex to tech layoffs.
3. It mixes high-frequency headlines with slower structural themes, giving both watchlist alerts and weekly research prompts.
4. It has large distribution and a clear conversion surface through performance-report and subscription links.

## Why The User Should Care

This account is useful for a daily macro dashboard. It supplies high-signal prompts for liquidity, rates, debt, energy, AI infrastructure, labor markets, and cross-asset flows. Every numerical claim should be verified against primary datasets or official releases before being promoted into investment work.
