# x-creator-swyx

Use this skill to track `@swyx`, AI Engineer, Latent Space, Cognition, Temporal, DX tips, Codex-as-workspace-tool, model price/performance benchmarks, production AI engineering workshops, product taste, long-term games, and AI community signals.

## Capture Workflow

1. Open `https://x.com/swyx` in Safari through Computer Use.
2. Capture profile metadata and linked affiliations first.
3. Classify each visible item as `operating_principle`, `product_request`, `tool_substitution`, `model_benchmark`, `production_case_study`, `community_event`, `developer_tool`, or `reply_followup`.
4. Preserve source URL, visible metrics, media/link hints, and quoted accounts.
5. Convert the post into an operator implication and a local Jarvis experiment.

## Extraction Fields

- Tool/model: Codex, ChatGPT, Grok, DevinAI, tldraw, Slack search, Mac utility.
- Benchmark dimension: intelligence per dollar, efficiency, source date, comparison set.
- Workflow: workspace search, image-input generation, production AI walkthrough, developer debugging.
- Community graph: AI Engineer, Latent Space, Cognition, Temporal, DX tips, Braintrust, Trainline.
- Product taste: concrete UX gap, build request, prototype target, adoption proof.

## Output Format

Return:

| Field | Content |
|---|---|
| Source | post URL and capture surface |
| Claim | one sentence summary |
| Evidence | visible text, media clue, metric, or linked source |
| Operator meaning | practical workflow, benchmark, or product implication |
| Jarvis experiment | local task or test that could validate the signal |
| Watch graph | accounts, products, events, and links to monitor next |

## Failure Modes

- Treating casual posts as throwaway comments and missing the embedded product request or benchmark frame.
- Copying model ranking claims without source date, evaluation dimension, and comparison set.
- Missing affiliated accounts as part of the monitoring graph.
- Ignoring replies that clarify the author's interpretation of a chart or benchmark.
- Treating community/event posts as social updates without extracting production workflow leads.
